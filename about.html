About Me

Learn about my journey, expertise, and the passion that drives wustackfullstack LLC.

Shixi Wu, Founder & Lead Developer

Hi! I’m Shixi Wu, the founder of WuStack Fullstack LLC. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer and Information Technology from the University of Pennsylvania, where I developed a deep passion for full-stack web development. From frontend design to backend infrastructure, I enjoy building robust, scalable, and elegant solutions that solve real-world problems.

Interestingly, I began my academic journey in finance during my undergraduate years. This unique combination of finance and computer science gives me an edge in understanding both technical architecture and business logic. It also helps me relate to those who are completely new to coding — because I once was, too. I know how overwhelming it can be to dive into the world of programming without a tech background, and that’s why I’m passionate about making complex concepts accessible and empowering others to build confidently from scratch.

Over time, I’ve built expertise in a wide range of technologies including JavaScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, and AWS. Whether it’s designing intuitive user interfaces or architecting secure APIs, I bring both detail-oriented craftsmanship and high-level strategic thinking to every project.

At WuStack Fullstack LLC, I offer full-cycle software development and technical consulting tailored to individual clients and small businesses. I take pride not just in writing clean code — but in delivering results that are functional, efficient, and future-proof.

If you’re looking for someone who bridges technical skill with clear communication and a deep understanding of learners' needs, I’d love to work with you.
